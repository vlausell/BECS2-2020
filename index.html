BUSINESS ENGLISH:
COMMUNICATON SKILLS
Part 2

BUSINESS ENGLISH: COMMUNICATION SKILLS is a course aimed at university students with an upper-intermediate to advanced level of English (between First Certificate and Proficiency) who are interested in eventually working in international business. The topics that make up the course have been selected considering their relevance in today’s international business environment. The aims of the course are to:
- enable the student to carry out a wide range of communicative tasks within a professional and international context similar to one in which he or she may one day be working,
- improve the students’ reading and listening comprehension through the use of authentic recordings and texts,
- introduce the vocabulary and register necessary to function within a professional context,
- review and build on essential English grammar, going over most problematic areas of grammar and syntax of the language used in business,
- develop students’ strategies for taking control of their own learning.
